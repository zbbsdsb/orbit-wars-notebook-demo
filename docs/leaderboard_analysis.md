## Key Observations

1. **Large score gap**: From entry-level 600 to top 1600+, the gap is over 2.5x
2. **600-point bottleneck**: Multiple discussions mention agents getting stuck at 600 points; specific strategies are needed to break through
3. **Heuristic method ceiling**: Pure heuristic methods can reach approximately 1000-1200 points
4. **RL method advantage**: Reinforcement learning methods like PPO can reach 1200+ points
5. **2P vs 4P**: The leaderboard includes both 2-player and 4-player matches; strategies need to accommodate both modes
